About

I am a researcher at BRAC University working at the intersection of semiconductor device physics and hardware-efficient compute architectures. My current research focuses on simulating sub-5 nm multi-bridge-channel transistors (MBCFETs) using Silvaco TCAD, alongside designing reconfigurable, hardware-efficient neural accelerators on FPGA fabric using Vitis HLS.

Beyond device modeling and digital design, I apply these technologies to aerospace and embedded systems—previously leading the avionics and ground station engineering for a 3U CubeSat mission, and developing locomotion and autonomy pipelines for robotic platforms.
